# Rezdora Training Library: Paid Pilot Proposal

## Simple Positioning

Rezdora Training Library is a private staff training tool for helping the team learn current menu, beverage, and service information faster.

The goal is to give managers one organized place to keep training material and give staff one easy place to study.

## What Rezdora Gets During The Pilot

- Private training website for Rezdora staff
- Organized food, wine, cocktail, and SOP study docs
- Searchable training library
- Role-based structure for staff and manager-facing information
- Quiz mode for staff knowledge checks
- Updates when menu, beverage, or SOP information changes
- Ongoing support from Emmanuel during the pilot

## Current Content Areas

- Current BTG wines
- Wine pairing wines
- Cocktails
- Dinner antipasti
- Future food sections
- Future SOP sections
- Future manager-only docs

## Pilot Scope

The pilot should stay focused.

Included:

- Keep the existing training site live
- Add and update Rezdora training docs
- Organize docs into sections and folders
- Keep quiz mode working for relevant content
- Collect feedback from managers and staff
- Improve the library based on actual use

Not included yet:

- Full custom mobile app
- Full HR system
- Payroll or POS integrations
- Enterprise reporting
- Automatic translation
- Multi-location support

## Suggested Pilot Length

30 to 60 days.

This is long enough to see whether the team actually uses it before building heavier features.

## Suggested Pricing

Option 1: Simple monthly pilot

- $199 per month
- Includes hosting, updates, and support

Option 2: Setup plus monthly support

- $500 setup fee
- $150 per month after launch

Option 3: Higher-touch version

- $750 setup fee
- $250 per month
- Includes more frequent content updates and manager feedback sessions

## What Emmanuel Owns

Emmanuel owns the software, code, structure, templates, and product direction.

## What Rezdora Owns

Rezdora owns its restaurant-specific content, including internal menu notes, SOPs, staff-facing training language, and any private business information it provides.

## Access If The Pilot Ends

If the pilot ends:

- Rezdora can receive an export of its own content.
- Emmanuel keeps the software and code.
- Access to the hosted training site ends unless a new agreement is made.

## Why This Is Valuable

Managers can:

- Keep training material organized
- Update staff faster
- Reduce repeated explanations
- See what areas need better training

Staff can:

- Search quickly
- Study before service
- Review wine, cocktail, food, and SOP information in one place
- Use quizzes to check understanding
